User story format: "As a <user type>, I want to <some goal> so that I can <(optional) for some reason>"

As an Administrator, I want to view receipts of customers to verify the amount of products that are being sold.

As a Curator, I want to see a list of products so that I can choose the appropriate artists to hire for a workshop.

As Customer Support Manager, I want to view customer contact information so that I can send them surveys.

As a Store Manager, I want to a list of products so that I can do inventory.

As an Artist, I want to see a list of customers who sign up for my workshop.
